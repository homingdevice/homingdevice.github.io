Giving Web a Memory Cost its Users Privacy

3rd party cookies: seperate from the domain you are visiting and mostly found in a banner ad. allows for potential tracking of your browsing history (advertisers use this to give you relevant ads) so if you visit multiple pages/domains with the same ad network attached it will send this info back to the advertisers and they will connect the dots between your browsing to create a profile for you to more "accurately" market to you
